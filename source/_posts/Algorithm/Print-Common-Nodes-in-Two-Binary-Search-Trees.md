打印 BST 的共同节点.

Given two Binary Search Trees, find common nodes in them. In other words, find intersection of two BSTs.

input:
```text
     root1        root2
       5           10
     /  \         /  \
    1    10      7   20
   / \   /      / \
  0   4 7      4   9
         \
          5
```
output : 4 7 9 10
